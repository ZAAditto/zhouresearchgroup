What We Do

We develop precision agriculture technologies powered by AI, computer vision, remote sensing, and robotics — creating practical, field-deployable tools that help agricultural producers make data-driven decisions.

Artificial Intelligence

Deep learning models for automated detection, classification, and decision support in crop management.

Smart Applications

Smartphone apps and portable devices that bring AI-powered analysis directly into the hands of growers.

Remote Sensing

UAV and ground-based imaging systems for crop monitoring, phenotyping, and yield prediction.

Robotics & Automation

Autonomous platforms for field scouting, data collection, and precision agriculture operations.

Extension & Outreach

Training, demonstrations, and technology evaluation to accelerate adoption across Louisiana.

Quality Inspection

Computer vision systems for postharvest quality assessment and bruise detection.
