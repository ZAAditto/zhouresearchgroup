Join Our Lab

We are always looking for motivated individuals passionate about precision agriculture, artificial intelligence, computer vision, remote sensing, and robotics. If you are interested in joining our group, please send your CV and a brief statement of research interests to Dr. Zhou at czhou@agcenter.lsu.edu

Job Descriptions

🎓

Graduate Students (M.S. & Ph.D.)

We are seeking highly motivated graduate students to pursue M.S. or Ph.D. degrees in precision agriculture, agricultural AI, computer vision, remote sensing, or related fields. Students will have the opportunity to work on funded research projects, publish in top journals, and develop field-deployable tools.

Apply
🔬

Postdoctoral Researchers

Postdoctoral positions are available for researchers with expertise in deep learning, computer vision, agricultural robotics, or remote sensing. Candidates should have a Ph.D. in a related field and a strong publication record.

Apply
🌍

Visiting Scholars

We welcome visiting scholars who wish to collaborate on precision agriculture research. Visiting periods are flexible and can be arranged based on mutual research interests and project needs.

Apply
💼

Student Workers

Undergraduate and graduate student worker positions are available for assisting with field data collection, image annotation, lab experiments, and equipment maintenance. These positions offer hands-on experience with cutting-edge agricultural technologies.
